Arturia Keystep 37

Midi Keyboard / Sequencer / Arpeggiator / CC Messages

Central to this setup has always been some form of Midi Keyboard. Initially I was using the M-Audio Keystation 61, but found it was taking up too much room and didn't include the flexibility I was looking for in a Midi Controller. Also hoping to experiment with transposing sequences, I decided on the Keystep 37 (a step up from the common Keystep 32). It fit nicely between the Neutron & QWERTY, could send out up to 16 CC messages, could act as a Midi Passthrough device, and had a bunch of chord features that I hadn't realized. 37 keys does feel a bit limiting at times, and only a single CV instrument can be controlled at once. Otherwise, this keyboard has handled all my Midi needs flawlessly.
