Monome Norns Shield

A plasic enclosure of digital creativity, or Pandora's music box. You decide.

Lots of guitarists acquire a collection of pedals claiming that each contributes to "their sound". Though many of these pedals are based on analog hardware, most of the modern pedals are comprised of digital circuits and driven by software. After using VST effects for several years, I realized that all these pedals could be replicated fairly easily through code. Thus the Monome Norns found its way into my collection. However, guitar effects processing was only the beginning of this trip.
