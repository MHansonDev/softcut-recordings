Supercollider

Coded Synthesis & Audio Processing

Upon discovering the Monome Norns, the Supercollider platform because another fascinating rabbit hole. Though I've just scathed the surface of the endless possibilities, I've already created dozens of nonsense projects that never fail to keep me entertained. While I was able to get a grasp of Norns scripts using Lua, the engines themselves were always a bit of a mystery. Using the Norns Pedalboard script as a guide, I've begun the arduous task of being creative with Supercollider and make usable engines and effects.
