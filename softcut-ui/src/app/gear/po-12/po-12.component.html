PO-12 Rythm

A wrecking ball disguised as a calculator

Before quitting Instagram I continued to see these little calculator devices pop up in my feed. After doing a bit of research and looking for an excuse to get a drum machine, I chose the cheapest Pocket Operator ebay had to offer: the PO-12 Rythm. While it was quick to annoy my roommates, I couldn't help but to come up with quick jams whenever I had a minute to spare. I've seen some in the YouTube community dismiss these as inferior toys, but between the range of effects and a port to sync with other devices, that couldn't be farther from the truth. Audio out directly into a loud amp and this thing can bring down a house.
